We are thrilled to launch the brand-new Games from Québec — a revamped hub dedicated to showcasing the incredible depth, diversity, and talent of Québec's video game industry.

From indie darlings to globally celebrated AAA titles, this platform is your window into one of the world's most vibrant game development ecosystems. Explore our catalogue of games, discover the studios behind them, and find your next career opportunity in the industry.

This is only the beginning. In the months ahead, we'll be adding more games, more studios, developer interviews, and in-depth features on the people shaping Québec's gaming landscape. Stay tuned.
